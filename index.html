About Us

With over 15 years of experience between them, Directors, Chris & Ted's aim is to provide homes and customers with modern, energy and eco efficient solutions to their plumbing and heating requirements.

ECO Solutions is an independent services and maintenance company, specialising in all aspects of Plumbing and Gas Work. ECO have established themselves throughout the South East of England and with their rapid growth since 2017 are continuing to provide their professional services to new and existing highly satisfied customers.

With thousands of jobs completed each year, we provide quality services in rapidly responsive times!
